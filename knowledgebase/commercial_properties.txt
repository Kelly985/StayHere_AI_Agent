- **Upper Hill:** KSH 120-180 per sq ft/month

## Commercial Property Investment Analysis

### Return on Investment by Property Type

**Office Buildings:**
- **Grade A:** 7-10% annual yield
- **Grade B:** 9-12% annual yield
- **Suburban offices:** 10-14% annual yield

**Retail Properties:**
- **Premium malls:** 8-12% annual yield
- **Community centers:** 10-15% annual yield
- **Street retail:** 12-18% annual yield

**Industrial/Warehouse:**
- **Modern facilities:** 10-15% annual yield
- **Specialized storage:** 12-18% annual yield
- **Manufacturing space:** 11-16% annual yield

### Market Trends 2024

**Growth Sectors:**
1. **E-commerce fulfillment centers** - 20% growth in demand
2. **Medical facilities** - 15% expansion in major towns
3. **Modern office spaces** - Flight to quality, 10% premium
4. **Cold storage facilities** - 25% increase in agricultural areas

**Declining Sectors:**
1. **Traditional retail** - Competition from online shopping
2. **Older office buildings** - Lack of modern amenities
3. **Basic warehousing** - Need for specialized facilities

### Investment Considerations

**Due Diligence Factors:**
- Zoning compliance and permits
- Accessibility and transport links
- Utility availability and costs
- Market demand and competition
- Anchor tenant quality and lease terms

**Financing Options:**
- **Commercial mortgages:** 12-16% interest rates, 60-70% LTV
- **Development finance:** 14-18% rates, 50-60% LTV
- **REITs participation:** 8-12% expected returns
- **Private equity/partnerships:** Varies by structure

**Risk Factors:**
- Economic volatility affecting demand
- Currency fluctuation for international tenants
- Regulatory changes and compliance costs
- Infrastructure development delays
- Competition from new developments
