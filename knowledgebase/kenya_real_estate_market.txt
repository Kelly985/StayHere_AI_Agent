- **Nyali:** 3-bedroom houses KSH 15-35 million, rentals KSH 80,000-200,000

## Infrastructure Impact on Property Values

### Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) Corridor
Properties within 10km of SGR stations show:
- 15-25% price premiums
- Higher rental demand
- Faster appreciation rates

**Key SGR Stations Impact:**
- **Syokimau:** 20% property value increase
- **Athi River:** 18% appreciation since SGR
- **Machakos:** 15% growth in surrounding areas
- **Mtito Andei:** Emerging investment destination

### Major Highway Corridors

**Thika Superhighway:**
- Properties along the highway: 25-40% value increase
- Juja, Ruiru, Thika showing sustained growth
- New satellite towns emerging

**Nairobi-Nakuru Highway:**
- Limuru, Uplands, Naivasha benefiting
- 10-20% property appreciation along corridor

**Mombasa Road:**
- Athi River, Machakos, Kitengela development
- Industrial and residential mixed growth

### Planned Infrastructure Projects

**Lamu Port-South Sudan-Ethiopia-Transport (LAPSSET) Corridor:**
- **Lamu:** Beachfront plots KSH 2-10 million per acre
- **Isiolo:** Logistics hub development, early investment opportunity
- Projected 30-50% property appreciation over 5-10 years

**Konza Technopolis:**
- Kenya's Silicon Valley project
- Current land prices: KSH 5-15 million per acre
- Projected completion phases driving investment

## Market Challenges and Considerations

### Common Issues
- **Title deed delays** - 6-24 months processing time
- **Infrastructure deficits** - Water, electricity, roads in emerging areas
- **Financing constraints** - High interest rates (12-16%)
- **Regulatory compliance** - NEMA, county approvals

### Due Diligence Requirements
- Verify land ownership at Ministry of Lands
- Check county zoning and development plans
- Confirm utility availability and costs
- Assess accessibility and transport links
- Review security situation and community development

### Investment Returns by Property Type
- **Residential rentals:** 4-8% annual yield
- **Commercial properties:** 8-12% annual yield
- **Industrial properties:** 10-15% annual yield
- **Agricultural land:** 5-10% appreciation + agricultural returns
- **Tourism properties:** 8-20% yield (seasonal variations)
